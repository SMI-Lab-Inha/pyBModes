# Code of Conduct

pyBmodes is developed by the Marine Structural Mechanics and Integrity Lab (SMI Lab) at Inha University and is open to contribution from the wider wind-energy research community. We adopt the [Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/) as our community standard.

## Our pledge

Members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in this project a respectful, welcoming experience for everyone, regardless of background, identity, or experience level. We commit to acting in ways that contribute to an open, professional, and collegial research community.

## Expected behaviour

- Demonstrate empathy and kindness toward others.
- Be respectful of differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences.
- Give and gracefully accept constructive technical feedback.
- Take responsibility for our mistakes, apologise to those affected, and learn from the experience.
- Focus on what is best for the project and the wider community, not just for ourselves.

## Unacceptable behaviour

Behaviour that would violate the [Contributor Covenant v2.1 standards](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/#our-standards) is not tolerated in any project space. This includes — but is not limited to — personal or political attacks, harassment of any form, publishing others' private information without permission, and any conduct that would reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional research setting.

## Enforcement responsibilities

The project maintainer is responsible for clarifying and enforcing this Code of Conduct and will take appropriate, fair corrective action in response to any behaviour deemed inappropriate, threatening, offensive, or otherwise harmful.

The project maintainer has the right and responsibility to remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, wiki edits, issues, and other contributions that are not aligned with this Code of Conduct, and will communicate reasons for moderation decisions when appropriate.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces — the GitHub repository (issues, pull requests, discussions, code review comments), any project-affiliated communication channel, and any public space where an individual is representing the project or its community.

## Reporting

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behaviour may be reported to the project maintainer:

**Jae Hoon Seo** — `jaehoon.seo@inha.ac.kr`

All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly. The project maintainer is obligated to respect the privacy and security of the reporter of any incident.

## Enforcement guidelines

Project maintainers will follow the [Contributor Covenant Enforcement Guidelines](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/#enforcement-guidelines) in determining the consequences for any action they deem in violation of this Code of Conduct. The four-tier ladder (correction → warning → temporary ban → permanent ban) is summarised at that link.
